Optimized Raptors Roster

Description: This data visualization features which players will optimize the Raptors roster, according to my model. It presents the performance statistics and salary of the players, accompanied by key team performance indicators (KPI).

Tip: To analyze how different combinations of players will impact the team's key performance indicators, try adding or removing players from the roster. To remove a player, deselect them from the filter panel, located on the right side of the dashboard. To add a player to the roster, search for them on the filter panel and select their name. For example, try deselecting Meyers Leonard and adding Serge Ibaka to the roster. 

Conclusion: According to my model, the free agents the Raptors should target are Christian Wood, Dwight Howard, and Meyers Leonard. Interestingly, the other player the model selected was Fred VanVleet, which is essentially suggesting that the team should re-sign him.

The payroll of the Raptors, with the 4 model suggested players, would be approximately $85 million ($112 million, including Siakam's $27 million raise for the 2020-2021 season).
