Flix Recommender

Netflix Film Recommender

Jupyter Notebook      Github Repo     

Description: FlixRecommender is my version of Netflix's recommender system. It uses an NLP (Natural Language Processing) model and a K-Means Clustering model to group Netflix movies and TV shows by their plot description, genre, lead actor or actress, director, and the country the movie or TV show was filmed in. Users can utilize my recommender model to find movies or TV shows that are similar to their favourite films.
