Basketize Correlations

Description: This data visualization presents team statistics highly correlated with winning in the 2019-2020 season.

Tip: To analyze how specific teams differ in terms of key performance metrics, try adding or removing teams from the filter panel, located on the right side of the dashboard. To specifically view how winning teams differ, move the W/L% (win-loss percentage) slider, located on the bottom-right corner of the dashboard. For example, try setting the minimum W/L% to 0.50.

Conclusion: Winning teams typically score a lot of points (PTS), make a lot of 3-pointers (3P), shoot efficiently (eFG% and 3P%), get a lot of defensive rebounds (DRB), and hold opposing teams to very few points (DRtg).

The Houston Rockets make the most 3-poitners per game (15.4) and the Miami Heat have the highest 3-point percentage (0.383). The Milwaukee Bucks score the most points per game (118.6) with the highest effective field goal percentage (0.553), and get the most defensive rebounds per game (42.2).
