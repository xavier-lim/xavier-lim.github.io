Basketize

Optimizing Basketball Rosters

LinkedIn Article    Dashboards        GitHub Repo   

Description: Basketize is a model I created to optimize an NBA roster based on their team statistics and team payroll. My model suggests the Toronto Raptors should re-sign Fred VanVleet and consider signing Christian Wood and Dwight Howard in the 2020 offseason. The dashboards below present how I developed the model and why I believe the Raptors should consider these players.

Basketize Interactive Dashboards

Optimized Raptors Roster

Displays the optimal Raptors roster according to my model, accompanied by their collective salary and key team statistics.
                                                                                                                

NBA Roster Payroll Breakdown

Displays the upcoming free agents and signed players for any NBA team, accompanied by their team payroll.



Optimized Dream Roster

Displays my optimal dream team roster according to my model, accompanied by their key team statistics.



Correlations

Analyzes the relationship between various team statistics and winning.
